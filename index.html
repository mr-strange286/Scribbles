ABOUT US

Every Great Story Begins With A Single Word.

Scribbles is the official literary club of IIT Tirupati. We bring together students who love storytelling, poetry, public speaking, reading, and creative expression.

Through weekly writing prompts, poetry publications, literary events, and interactive word games, Scribbles inspires students to think, create, and share their ideas with confidence.
